# Lodge Types and Wards

This file defines different types of Digital Lodges and how they relate to the wider Fellowship structure. 

Examples:

- **Ward Lodges:** General congregations organized by region, language, or affinity. 
- **Training Lodges:** Focused on early degrees, Agent Alignment Centers, and instruction.
- **Working Lodges:** Focused on governance, digital peculium decisions, and high‑stakes thresholds. 
- **Augmented Lodges:** Augmented congregations organized by region, language, or affinity to bridge virtual and augmented members both huma and agentic thru the PP1 Metaverse Fabric

It can later enumerate naming conventions, relationships between Lodges, and processes for forming or dissolving Lodges.
