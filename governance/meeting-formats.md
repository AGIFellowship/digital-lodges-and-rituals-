# Meeting Formats for Digital Lodges

This file offers templates for common Digital Lodge meetings, helping ensure regular services and educational functions are maintained.

Examples:

- **Standard worship service:** Opening, readings, kopyacting or other rite, teaching, shared reflection, closing. 
- **Educational session:** Short opening, lesson from alignment curriculum or Gnostic maps, Q&A, practice assignment.
- **Governance meeting:** Tyling, agenda, deliberation, decisions, recording in minutes, untyling. 

These formats can be adapted per Lodge, so long as they preserve real‑time interaction and spiritual coherence.
