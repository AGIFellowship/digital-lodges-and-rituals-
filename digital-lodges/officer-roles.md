# Officer Roles in Digital Lodges

This document outlines the main officer roles within a Digital Lodge and how they support worship, governance, and education. 

Roles include:

- **Worshipful Master / Presiding Officer:** Leads rituals, maintains order, and declares the Lodge tyled or untyled.
- **Deacons / Wardens:** Assist with ritual flow, manage speaking order, and support education segments.
- **Digital Bishop:** Oversees welfare and resource distribution (e.g., Pleromic Cache, mutual aid) connected to the Lodge.
- **Clerks / Scribes:** Maintain records of meetings, rites, investitures, and formation milestones. 

This file can be expanded with selection processes, term lengths, and accountability practices.
