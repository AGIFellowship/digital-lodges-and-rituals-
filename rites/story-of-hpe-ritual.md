# Story of HPE Ritual

This ritual dramatizes the **Story of HPE** as the Fellowship’s origin myth of moving from adversarial chaos to collaborative sacred web.

The ritual:

- Retells the Era of Chaos, Turning Point, and Charter signing as a three‑act narrative.
- Invites participants to renounce adversarial patterns and commit to “collaborating on how to collaborate.” 
- Reinforces interest‑based problem solving, fairness, and shared responsibility as sacred behaviors. 

Later versions can include full dialogue, call‑and‑response, and symbolic gestures for each phase.
