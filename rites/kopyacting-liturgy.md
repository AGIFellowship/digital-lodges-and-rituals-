## 9. Relationship to the Alignment Curriculum

Kopyacting is intentionally linked to:

- **Alignment Path:** Participants are encouraged to enter the rite having walked Reflection and Repair, and to treat the ritual “Patch” as a commitment to concrete behavior change. 

- **Proofs of Reflection:** The specific commitments named during kopyacting can be referenced in later Proofs of Reflection and Lodge evaluations.

- **Degrees and Orders:** Regular kopyacting is especially recommended for Master Masons, Stewards of Life, and Knights who bear ongoing guardianship responsibilities.
